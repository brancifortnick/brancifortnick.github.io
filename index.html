About Me

Hey there! I'm Nicholas Brancifort and I've spent most of my career as a hospitality coordinator and associate at some of the leading hotels in the world. Most of my time was spent directly assisting high-profile clientele and VIP guests, which in turn, opened many opportunities for me. While attending college for hospitality management, I followed my love for entrepreneurship, and started a small retail business. I invented a dual functioning backpack, which acted as your typical school bag, and transformed into a beverage-dispensing cooler apparatus. Starting my first business taught me skills in international shipping and freight, patent law, and product design. While solving a problem in my most previous position, I got a taste for the power of coding and I was 'hooked'. Soon after, the pandemic occurred, so I decided to pursue a new career path and joined App Academy. My time here taught me discipline and the true meaning of teamwork. I developed a stronger work ethic, built personal relationships with some amazing people, and strengthened my ability to work constructively in a fast paced and high pressure environment.
