About Me

Hey there! I'm Nicholas Brancifort and I've focused much of my time and career as a hospitality coordinator and employee at different leading hotels of the world in an array of selective brands. Most of my time was spent directly assisting high-profile clientele and VIP guests, which opened doors and opportunities for me. While attending college for hospitality management, I followed my love for entrepreneurship, and started a small retail business. I invented a dual functioning backpack, which acted as your typical school bag, and transformed into a beverage-dispensing cooler apparatus. Starting this business taught me skills in international shipping and freight, patent law, product design, and business do's and dont's. Unexpectedly solving a technical computer error while at work - I got a glimpse of coding and I was 'hooked'. The pandemic followed shortly, so I decided to pursue academics, and I was accepted to App Academy. My time here taught me discipline and the true meaning of teamwork. I developed a stronger, smarter work ethic, built personal relationships with some amazing people, and strengthened my ability to work constructively in a fast paced and high pressure environment.
