About Me

I was born and raised in New Jersey, though I traveled often from a young age. I developed a keen interest in hospitality and service from my parents, who both worked in the industry. Throughout my formative years, I excelled academically and displayed a natural aptitude for the service industry and business aspects that surround it. During my time obtaining my high school education I began work at Wendy's, where I gained exposure in assisting and serving patrons, which ultimately helped me in developing communication skills. This foundational period instilled in me a strong work ethic and a passion for service and hospitality. Driven by a desire to broaden my horizons, I transitioned to The Rittenhouse Hotel where I thrived in roles such as In room dining server/coordinator and eventually supervisor. Here, I was fortunate to contribute to 2 continuous 5 diamond awards. This reinforced my commitment to learn, master my craft, and deliver exceptional service. Beyond my professional endeavors, I am deeply engaged in software and music engineering and development. These pursuits allowed me to explore my creativity and passion for innovation, which lead me to starting my own business during my college studies. My dedication to these projects reflects my belief in the importance of getting a difficult task completed, sticking with it, and seeing it through to the end. Looking ahead to now, I am eager to continue my journey of growth and impact in Hospitality Management and Tourism, I remain inspired by my Mother and Father, whose achievements motivate me to strive for excellence and make a positive impact in the world. Outside of work, I enjoy time with friends, traveling to new places, and meeting new people. This helps provide me with a positive work life balance. These pursuits enrich my life and inform my approach to both personal and professional challenges. I am grateful for the opportunities I have had thus far and look forward to the exciting chapters that lie ahead.

Medley

Medley

For the first time during this solo full-stack project I used AWS s3 buckets for song and image storage along with trying out a flask backend , react front end and of course a redux store. Users can upload their favorite artists and audio files along with profile images, edit, delete, and create biographies about the artist, and browse the community. The user has the ability to play music directly from the site in comparison to my first project 'Songs Smarter' where I chose to redirect the user off site to listen.
